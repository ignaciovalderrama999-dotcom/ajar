# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want ajar to be a welcoming, harassment-free project for everyone, regardless
of experience, background, or identity.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that helps the community:

- Being respectful and constructive in issues, PRs, and discussions.
- Welcoming newcomers and helping them contribute.
- Keeping the project **defensive**: ajar exists to help people protect their own
  software, never to attack.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Sharing others' private information without consent.
- Using the project, its issues, or its community to plan, request, or promote
  unauthorized or malicious activity.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and comments that violate
this code, and may block repeat offenders. To report an issue, contact the
maintainer (add your preferred contact here).

This Code of Conduct is adapted in spirit from the
[Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/).
